How To Find Your Guide

Your guide will be waiting for you at the meeting point, right next to the statue of Charlemagne on the Parvis de Notre-Dame.

To make them easy to spot, they will be holding a sign with the "SZ" logo.

Please plan to arrive 5-10 minutes early to ensure a prompt start to your tour. We look forward to meeting you!
